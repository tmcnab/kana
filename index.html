About

This is is a simple application to help you practice your kana skills. The corpus was extracted from the Wiktionary (CC BY-SA 3.0) and purports to be 1000 common Japanese words.

To use this tool you simply type the corresponding kana into the textbox and hit return. Used often enough you will learn through repetition and will not need audio cues to read kana.

Keyboard Shortcuts

escape skip current word
ctrl+escape speak current word
